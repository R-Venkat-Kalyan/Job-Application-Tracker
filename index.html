Why should I track job applications?

Tracking helps you stay organized and follow up on opportunities, especially when applying to multiple companies across various platforms.

What details should I log for each job?

Include company name, job title, application date, JD link, resume used, and platform you applied through (e.g., LinkedIn, Naukri).

How often will I get reminders?

You will receive weekly reminders for jobs that haven't had a status update and monthly summaries for your overall job hunt progress.

Can I access my tracker on mobile?

Yes! Our platform is fully responsive, so you can view and manage your applications from any device, anytime, anywhere.
